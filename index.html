Studio

What we can print for you

Classic Colour produces catalogues, leaflets, flyers, business cards, letterheads, brochures, posters, point of sale, direct mail packs. We also print on plastic, for instance, pre-paid, gift, discount, telephone, loyalty, membership or information cards. These can be produced in PVC, Polypropylene, HIPs or polyester. We have onsite facilities for the production of prestige packaging with window patching, bespoke CD, DVD and Blu Ray tray placement and in-line folding and gluing.

What we offer

Under one roof, we can offer a comprehensive design, artwork, print, and finishing service; extensive mailing capability; warehousing along with stock management and managed distribution.

Our ethos

To supply top quality print you need state of the art equipment and operating systems. But it also calls for something more; for skilled and experienced people, who take pride in their trade and care about every detail of every job. It also requires a company-wide commitment to fully support every client, no matter what size their project. We provide unparalleled assistance, advice and guidance to all our clients. We are mindful of our own impact on the environment and would be pleased to help you improve yours.
